ABOUT THIS WEBSITE

WEBSITE OVERVIEW

The website has been created for diabetics and patients can access it through the mobile app port of the website. In the app patients can record their daily test data and comments and upload them to clinician, view their data and see how their data ranks against everyone else, and also receive some support messages.
